### 🔢 Two Key Percentage Formulas:

1. **Percentage = (x / y) × 100**
   👉 Used to find **what percent x is of y**

2. **Percentage Change = ((x − y) / y) × 100**
   👉 Used to find **percentage increase or decrease** from `y` to `x`


---
